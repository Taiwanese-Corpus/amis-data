ta'og - 1. to bow * showing respect 鞠躬，表示敬意 2. to salute
* to nod to 敬禮，行禮 3. to worship 崇拜，敬拜，禮拜

bow a lot. 日本人善於點頭行禮
Ta'ogen ko sigsi iso. Show respect to our teacher (by some
physical sign) . 向你的老師敬禮，你要尊敬你的老師

主日禮拜

temple. 這是敬拜的殿

worship of God is the beginning of wisdom. 敬畏神是智慧的開
端
