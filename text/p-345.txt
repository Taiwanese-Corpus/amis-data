Latoloen nira a pasacfacafag cagra. － He put them in three
groups. 他把他們編為三組
O kalatolo / kalitolo konini. － This is one third. 這是三分之一
tolo' － 1. to fall literally 跌倒 2. to fall figuratively 比喻上的，倒
下，落下，跌落
Aka ka tolo'. － Don't fall down. 不要跌倒
Matolo' ko faloco' ako a minegneg to dmak nira. － My heart
(i.e. I) stumbled seeing what he did. 我看到他的行為就隨之犯罪
tolok － a China Pheasant ＊ a large game bird ＊Swinhoe's
Pheasant 皿腹[困鳥]
tolon －1. to pray＊ a prayer 禱告，祈禱 2. to carry a command
or request to someone 信息，叮嚀，吩咐或請託某人
O pitolon ko ga'ay. － You should pray. 最好還是禱告
O maan ko tatolonen? －What should we pray for? 我們為何事禱
告？
Mitolon kako to saki tisowanan. － I am praying for you. 我為你禱
告
Patolon cigra to sowal i takowanan. － He gave me a message to
pass on. 他託我帶話
tomadaw － to look over and check work done 檢查和審驗工作
tomay － 1. a bear 熊 2. to be fierce 兇猛 ＊ violent 兇暴
Matomay cigra. － He is fierce. 他很兇，他粗暴，他撒野
tomcek － to focus on one thing only 瞪眼，目不轉睛，凝視
Patomcek sa cigra a somowal takowanan. － He directed his
words to me. 他瞪眼看著我
lcowa ko patomcekan iso? － Where is your focus point? (in
choosing a person) 你的目標在那裡？
Cima ko katomcekan iso? － Whom are you considering? 你在顧
慮誰呀！你在關心什麼人？
tomes － to fill ＊ to fill up 使滿，充滿，裝滿，填滿 ＊ with liter-
al and figurative meanings 含字面上和比喻上的滿
Tomesen ko pawti. － Fill the sack. 把麻袋盛滿
Matomes ko kaysig. － The dish is full 碗裝得滿滿的
Matomes no lipahak ko faloco' ako. － My heart is filled with
joy. 我心充滿喜樂
