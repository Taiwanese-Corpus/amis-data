tagic - 1. cry * expressed or unexpressed * grief 哭泣 2. to
beseech 哀求，懇求
O tomagicay cagra. - They are crying. 他們在哭泣

expressed) . 我內心在悲泣

of everyone. 不要在眾人面前哭泣
Aka patagicen kako. - Don't beseech / try to force me. 不要勉強
我
Tanotagic sa ci Tafiti a macokacok. - David wept as he went up
hill. 大衛一直哭著爬山
