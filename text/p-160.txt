




ladiw

ladiw / radiw. song ＊ singing ＊ music 歌，唱歌，音樂
Lomadiw kita. - Let's sing. 我們來唱歌
Ladiw sa cagra tahadadaya. －They kept singing till evening. 我
們唱到晚上
ladom / radom. to carry water from a well or other water supply
to the home 提水，挑水，汲水
lafac / rafac. (an animal) being loose and free to roam (動物) 飛
掉或溜掉
Palafacen. - Let the animal go free. 放走，釋放，把動物放生
lafades / pades. trouble ＊ to be tested ＊ to have trials lasting
for a long time 困難，苦難，痛苦，試煉，受折磨
lafag - 1. guests 客人 2. patrons 顧客 3. strangers *
outsiders 異鄉人，陌生人 4. visitors 訪客
Palafag kita. - Let's go on a trip. 我們去做客 (旅行)
sapalafag a tahka' - provision of a meal for guests 為宴客預備的
佳餚
kalilafagan - living room * guest room 客廳，客房
lafay / rafay. an earthen vessel for storing vegerabes 陶器菜
盤 ＊carried on one's head to the fields 到田裡頂在頭上的容器
lafel - to let run loose * roam 落掉，溜掉，流走
Malafelay ko siri niyam. - Our sheep are loose / not penned
up. 我們的羊跑掉了
lafi - dinner * the evening meal 晚餐
misakalafi - prepare dinner 煮晚餐，預備晚餐
pisakalafian ko cidal - late afternoon 傍晚
Malafi kita. －Let's eat dinner. 我們一起吃晚餐
palafi to wawa - give the child supper 給孩子吃晚餐
kalafian - dinner time 晚餐時間
lafii - the middle of the night * midnight 午夜，深夜，子夜，半夜
misalafii a matayal－to work through the night 趕夜工 (熬夜)
Salafii sa cigra a minokay. －He reaceled home in the middle of
the night. 他徹夜回家
lafii ko sakatahira nira i loma'. - He arrived home at midnight.
他半夜抵達家
lafin - to stay for a short period overnight or several nights 過夜
