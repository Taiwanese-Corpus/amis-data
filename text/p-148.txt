kiyko / Kiyko. name of an Amis village 花蓮溪口村部落名
kiyo - eggplant * a vegetable 茄菜
Kiyot - to dig out * scratch off something small like a spot of
dirt 挖出，鑿出，刪除，像刪除小斑點的東西
klac  - to chip at ＊ cut out ＊ as wood or a rotten spot in
fruit 削, 切
klalaci - a flowering shrub called Bleeding Heart Glorybower (Cler -
dendron thomsonae) 龍吐珠
klap - to scoop out ＊ dig out rice seedlings 剷除，鏟出
saklap - 1. a scoop for digging 鏟子 2. a spatula 調刀，小鏟，
壓台器
kleg - a man - hole 出入孔，迴避孔
misakleg - to make a man - hole to cause someone to fall in 做一
出入孔, 使人能陷進
tahakleg - to fall in a man - hole 掉入洞中
klet - to use a sickle to cut off twine 割，收割大麻的過程
kli' - 1. the sound of clinking or a creaking sound 金屬物品掉落之
聲響 2. the sounds of a thief 小偷偷東西的聲響
klig - 1.the sound of a bell or phone or clock 鈴聲，電話聲，鐘
聲　2. to fry or roast in a pan 煎，炸，烘焙
kliw - 1. thread 線 (亞麻) the plant from which string or thread
is made * China Grass - Ramie (Boehmeria frutescens) 青苧麻
hanakliw - a wild plant similar to kliw but not used for making
cloth 野生植物，類似青苧麻，但不用來做衣料
klog - 1. a resounding noise as to hear a door being closed 開，關
門之碰聲　2. a man - hole 出入孔
kltan - lower back below the waist and to the sides 腰部
kma - 1. slow 緩慢 * as an ox pulling a cart 如牛拖車 2. to be
numb with cold 因冷而麻木
kmi - pain in the chest experienced while breathing 呼吸引致胸部之
疼痛，肺炎
kmo - 1. on target * squarely on the mark 目標，鵠的 2. to fall
from a high place 從高處掉落
Pakmo han nira ko falohag ako. - He hit me squarely in the
chest. 他直接地擊打我的胸部
