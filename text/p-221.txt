patarod - to substitute an item or person for the original 儲備，後

是後補球員

替換原來已損壞的機械
patay - 1.death * to die 死 2. figurtatively to be crushed and
despair 比喻希望被粉碎和絕望 3. to go to extreme limits to do
something 極盡限制去做某些事情

Kinapatayto cigra. - He killed himself. 他自殺了
